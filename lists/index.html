Extra Cheesy Pepperoni Pizza Recipe

Ingredients:

For the pizza dough:

  • 1 ½ cups warm water (110°F to 115°F)
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt

For the pizza sauce:

  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For assembling the pizza:

  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup sliced pepperoni
  • Additional toppings (optional): sliced bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, etc.

Instructions:

1. Prepare the Dough:

  1. Combine warm water, yeast, and sugar in a small bowl. Let it sit for 5 minutes until frothy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour and salt. Make a well.
  3. Pour the yeast mixture and olive oil into the well. Stir until a dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough on a floured surface for 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  5. Place dough in a greased bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise for 1-1.5 hours until doubled in size.

2. Make the Pizza Sauce:

  1. Heat olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and sauté for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper.
  4. Simmer the sauce for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally. Adjust seasonings.

3. Preheat the Oven:

Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C) with a pizza stone or inverted baking sheet inside.

4. Prepare the Pizza:

  1. Punch down the risen dough and divide it in half.
  2. Roll out one portion of the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a piece of parchment paper.

5. Add Toppings:

  1. Spread half of the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a border for the crust.
  2. Sprinkle half of the mozzarella and Parmesan cheese over the sauce.
  3. Arrange the pepperoni slices and any other desired toppings.

6. Bake the Pizza:

  1. Carefully transfer the parchment paper with the assembled pizza onto the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet.
  2. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden brown, and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.

7. Repeat:

While the first pizza is baking, repeat the process with the second portion of dough and remaining toppings.

8. Serve:

Remove the pizzas from the oven and let them cool for a minute before slicing. Enjoy your extra cheesy pepperoni pizza!
