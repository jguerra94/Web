Frequently asked questions.

Are cameras allowed in the galleria?

A: No professional photo or video cameras of any kind will be permitted into the gallery without consent from the managing staff.

Are backpacks allowed in the galleria?

A: No backpacks or large handbags will be allowed in the gallery. Small hand clutch purses no bigger than 14 inches are acceptable. Small clear handbags are also acceptable.

Is food acceptable to bring inside the gallery?

To preserve the gallery's ambiance, we kindly ask guests not to bring outside food. Our in-house cafe offers coffee and snacks for those in need of a quick bite, with consumption limited to the cafe area. Your cooperation ensures an undisturbed and harmonious art appreciation experience for all.

Are water bottles permitted?

A: water bottles are accepted. The lid of the water bottle must remain closed when not being consumned. We also have a few water fountains to refill water bottles with and for those who need a drink.

Are cellphones permitted

A: Cellphones are acceptable to bring into the galleria. We ask that you please have your phone on silence mode as to not disturb the other guests enjoying the exhibits. If you need to take a call please go to the cafe or outside of the gallery. Cellphone pictures are permitted but please no flash photography in order to maintain the highest quality of our art works.
