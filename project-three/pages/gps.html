Directions

Nestled at the vibrant intersection of culture and sophistication, New York City Galleria awaits your presence at 1330 5th Avenue in the heart of Manhattan, just a stone's throw from the northeast entrance to the iconic Central Park. As you navigate our art-filled haven, you'll find us neighborly to the esteemed Locke School of Arts and Engineering, standing as our creative companion. Across the street, the towering Martin Luther King towers lend their majestic presence, while the New York City Fire Department's engine 28 stands sentinel just a block away. Positioned amidst these landmarks, we are an unmissable gem in the city's cultural crown. Accessing our sanctuary is a seamless endeavor, with multiple transportation options beckoning you. The subway from Grand Central also has a stop a mere block away, which is brief 3-minute stroll to our doors. Alternatively, indulge in the quintessential New York experience by hailing a yellow cab, where the drivers themselves are eager storytellers, sharing the rich tapestry of the city's history with curious travelers. For those seeking the pinnacle of convenience, if your sojourn brings you to the Marriott hotel half a mile away, consider yourself in luck. The hotel generously extends complimentary shuttle buses to and from our art haven—simply inquire at the front desk for further details. And for those planning a more extended stay in the city, indulge in our special accommodation offer—20% off a minimum two-night retreat at the Marriott.
